## üìä METRICS TO TRACK

### Success metrics (behavior, not growth):

1. **Completion rate per step**
   - How many users complete Step 1 ‚Üí 2 ‚Üí 3... ‚Üí 8
   - Where do users drop off?

2. **Time to first data entry**
   - Days from signup to first transaction logged
   - Faster = better awareness behavior

3. **Week 1 retention**
   - % users who log transactions 3+ days in first week
   - Core habit formation indicator

4. **Referral quality** (NOT quantity)
   - % of referred users who actually use the system
   - Quality > quantity

### ‚ùå DON'T track (vanity metrics):
- Total signups
- Viral coefficient
- Referral conversion rate
- Trial-to-paid conversion
