## ğŸ¯ WHEN TO USE EACH APPROACH

### **Use Conversion-First When:**

1. **Need revenue NOW**
   - Runway is short (<6 months)
   - Investors demand fast traction
   - Must prove monetization quickly

2. **Product is mature**
   - PMF is validated
   - Features are stable
   - Value prop is clear

3. **Market is competitive**
   - Users expect paid tier
   - Competitors are aggressive
   - Need to capture premium users fast

4. **FREE is intentionally limited**
   - FREE is truly a teaser
   - Premium is the real product
   - Upgrade is expected behavior

**Example:** Grammarly, Notion, Spotify
- FREE is clearly limited
- Premium is standard expectation
- Users know they'll need to upgrade

---

### **Use Value-First When:**

1. **Testing product-market fit**
   - New product/feature
   - Unclear value prop
   - Need usage insights

2. **Runway is sufficient**
   - 12+ months runway
   - Can afford slow growth
   - Prioritize learning over revenue

3. **Market is new/uncertain**
   - Users don't know they need this
   - Education phase required
   - Word-of-mouth critical

4. **FREE should be valuable**
   - FREE is a real product
   - Community is important
   - Long-term engagement > fast conversion

**Example:** Slack, Figma, Airtable (early days)
- FREE was genuinely useful
- Upgrades came from organic need
- Strong retention from Day 1
